Bosses:

You can begin to battle bosses when you meet the level requirements necessary for fighting a particular boss.

Bosses require 3 battle stock. Boss fights occur over several phases (similar to battles) where you have a chance of phase victory. You and the boss deal damage to one another at the end of the phase based on many conditions, primarily your higher stat between POW and WIS.

Your damage dealt is significantly reduced when you lose the phase. Damage dealt and damage received can also be heavily dependent on what actives you or the boss have. Beware of special boss actives! When either the HP of yourself or the boss drops to 0, the boss fight ends.

If you reached 0 HP first (or at the same time as the boss), you lose and don't receive any rewards. If you defeat the boss first, you have a chance at receiving loot, always receiving the first time drop if it's the first time you beat the boss (or if you sold/lost the first time drop) and possibly more based on your luck.

Bosses have an array of items for loot but it's also possible for a loot attempt to come up with nothing. The higher the luck, the more chances at loot attempts you get (but not the chance of receiving better loot)! Defeating bosses does not reward any experience, gold or class experience, so choose wisely whether you want to try your chance at boss loot or fight regular battles. The first time drop and other boss information can be seen using the '!grumbo boss info ' command.



List of available bosses can be found below:

Venom Grumbo

Lv Req: 30 | Command: venom_grumbo

HP 400 | POW 90 | WIS 94

Base Phase Victory Chance: 48%

Poisonous. Toxic. But most importantly, Venomous. Venom Grumbo administers poison to its victims and deals extra damage to them as well!

Actives: Venom, Venom Bite, Equalizer

Loot: Antidote, Medicine, Mini Magnet, Venom Plate, Venom Mask EX

First time drop: Venom Mask

Venom Actives:

Venom:Inflicts poison before phase results. If you have an antidote in your inventory, it will destroy an antidote instead of inflicting poison.

Venom Bite: Deals 3 extra damage. Multiplies total damage by 5 if the target is poisoned

Equalizer: Activates when below 120 HP. If you have less than POW 85 or WIS 85 or RES 8, receive damage equal to the difference between your POW and WIS. Only activates once.

Mixtape Grumbo

Lv Req: 45 | Command: mixtape_grumbo

HP 416 | POW 80 | WIS 146

Base Phase Victory Chance: 41%

Beware the super hot fire of the Mixtape Grumbo!

Actives: Show me your Mixtape, Disco Inferno, Final Track

Loot: Medicine, Bandages, Adrenaline, Class Ticket, Academy Jacket, Angel Wings

First time drop: Safety Boots

Mixtape Actives:

Show me your Mixtape: If you do not have (11 - phase #) actives, chance of phase victory and damage done is reduced by 64.

Disco Inferno:Deals 6 damage. From phase 4 onward, heals for the difference between his WIS and your WIS (heals for 0 if you have more). That same difference % chance to deal 60 damage.

Final Track:From phase 8 onward, deals 416 damage.

Crimson Grumbo

Lv Req: 60 | Command: crimson_grumbo

HP 620 | POW 116 | WIS 130

Base Phase Victory Chance: 38%

The red-eyed Crimson Grumbo. His crimson eyes make for some scary effects!

Actives: Red Eye, Rock Smash, Crimson Blood

Loot: Battle Potion, Feather Stone, Blood Potion, Crimson Staff, Crimson Scepter

First time drop: Crimson Dagger

Crimson Actives:

Red Eye: Activates in Phase 1 against Crimson Grumbo. Petrifies if you don't have a Feather Stone and a Battle Potion in your items. Does not consume the Feather Stone or Battle Potion.

Rock Smash:Activates in Phase 1 against Crimson Grumbo. If DEF > 150, receive an additional 100 damage. Otherwise, receive additional damage equal to DEF/10.

Crimson Blood: Activates in Phase 2 against Crimson Grumbo. Puts the Crimson Blood active on your character. (DEF/10 + RES*2)% chance of removing the active every battle/phase. Lasts 2 turns. On the second turn, if it's still active, receive an additional 100 damage.

Master Grumbo

Lv Req: 100 | Command: master_grumbo

HP 1000 | POW 160 | WIS 185

Base Phase Victory Chance: 50%%

The Master of many Grumbos. Only those who are acknowledged by the Master Grumbo have a chance at beating him!

Actives: Teaching, Strategize, Evaluation, Gate of Grumbo

Loot: Medicine, Battle Potion, Class Ticket, Adventurer's Spellbook, Grumbot Miner, Grumbo Whistle, Master Grumbo's Blessing

First time drop: Master Cloak

Master Actives:

Teaching:Master Grumbo's regular attack every phase. Deal an additional 10 damage. Reduced by a random number between 0 and your SPD. Can be reduced below 0.

Strategize:If the total bonus increases to your battle chance is less than 6%, reduce damage dealt by 100. Can heal the boss.

Evaluation:Every 4 phases, if the difference between your POW/WIS is less than 45, receive 45 damage. Otherwise, that same difference % chance to reduce your damage recieved by 15.

Gate of Grumbo:Every 5 phases, (SPD+LUK)*2 % chance for Master Grumbo to reduce your damage received by 50. Otherwise, Master Grumbo will reduce his damage received by 150. Can heal the boss.
